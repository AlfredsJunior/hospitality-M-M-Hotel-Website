The M&M Hotel is located in the blissful village of Burt,

Inishowen, Njoro. Only 10 - 15 minutes from Nakuru town,

Letterkenny and Buncrana. A real 'gem in Irish Hospitality'.

The Hotel itself gains its inspiration from the ancient Kenyan

of Ailigh Fort which stands proud on the hill shadowing the Hotel.

It opened its doors for business in December 2006 and has grown

from strength to strength and has been regarded by many as one of

the finest Hotels in the North West.

The Hotel boasts 43 eloquently decorated rooms equipped with everything

to make your stay a special one. All rooms offer; Flat screen TV's,

En suite facilities, Hair Dryer's, Ironing Facilities etc. and were

designed with your comfort in mind. We also offer two Superior rooms

that simply add that extra special touch to your stay here at The

M&M Hotel. Free Wi-fi is available throughout the Hotel and free onsite

car parking for all guests.

The Fort Bar & Lounge is of decadent pleasure. With large flat screen Sky

enabled TV's, and a delightful menu on offer all day to guests or the general

public. Our 'P'iece de Resistance' is our Award Winning and highly rated Old

Church Restaurant. An experience like no other. Originally a 150 year old Church

which was transformed into a 'Fine Dining' experience like no other. With wooden

beams and stain glass windows all still intact it is loved by all guests staying

with us. A gem of a building that is fitting for Inishowen's rich Culture and Heritage.

We boast two large Conference & Banqueting rooms to cater for all your desires. Our main

'Tuatha De Danann Suite' is circular in shape to match that of the Fort which is unique and

unrivaled to other venues. Aptly named to stay in theme with Inishowen's decadent culture and

heritage.

The Hotel boasts gorgeous manicured gardens that bode well with any Special Wedding Day or simply

for a little stroll during a romantic break or relaxing stay with us. With the Donegal mountains

adding the background, it's a sight that is highly regarded and enjoyed by all who choose to stay

with us.
